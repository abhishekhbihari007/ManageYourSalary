## How to Add Calculator Links to Articles

### Option 1: Add in Article Content (Markdown Format)
Add calculator links directly in the article content using markdown:

```markdown
## Use Our Calculator

[Calculate Your In-Hand Salary](/calculator/in-hand-salary) to see your exact take-home amount.

Or use our [Tax Regime Calculator](/calculator/tax-regime) to compare Old vs New regime.
```

### Option 2: Add Call-to-Action Section
Add a dedicated section at the end of each article:

```markdown
## Ready to Calculate?

- [In-Hand Salary Calculator](/calculator/in-hand-salary) - Calculate your take-home salary
- [Tax Regime Calculator](/calculator/tax-regime) - Compare Old vs New regime
- [EPF Calculator](/calculator/epf) - Project your retirement corpus
```

### Option 3: Add Calculator Links in Specific Sections
Add relevant calculator links where they're mentioned in the content:

```markdown
### Step 3: Calculate In-Hand Salary
In-Hand Salary = Gross Salary - All Deductions

**Use our [In-Hand Salary Calculator](/calculator/in-hand-salary) to calculate this automatically.**
```
